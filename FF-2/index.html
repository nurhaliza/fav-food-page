Grilled Cheese Sandwich

My Favorite Food by Collin Chiu

Where It's From

Grilled cheese sandwiches were popularized during the U.S. Great Depression era.

Ingredients

  • Your choice of cheese
  • Your choice of bread
  • Butter

How To Make

  1. Butter the toast
  2. Heat skillet and add bread
  3. Add cheese
  4. Keep grilling and flip when cooked
  5. Done!

Why It's My Favorite

Grilled cheese sandwiches are my favorite because it is a comfort home food for me :)
