Sushi Origin

Sushi is associated with the Japanese Cuisine.

It is often believed that Hanaya Yohei was the inventor of modern sushi.


Common Sushi Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Nori
  • Often Prepared With Filling:
    • Raw Seafood
      • Salmon
      • Tuna
      • Squid
      • Eel
      • Yellowtail
      • Imitation Crab Meat
      • Other Seafoods
    • Vegetables
      • Pickled Daikon Radish
      • Pickled Vegetables
      • Avocado
      • Cucumber
      • Asparagus
      • Other Vegetables
    • Tofu
    • Eggs

Instructions

  1. Lay a sheet of nori horizontally on top of the bamboo mat
  2. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice on top of the nori
  3. Add a thin layer of fillings of your choice by alligning it horizontally on top of the rice
  4. Roll up the nori tightly using the bamboo mat
  5. Using a sharp knife, cut the sushi vertically into bite sized pieces.
  6. Done!
